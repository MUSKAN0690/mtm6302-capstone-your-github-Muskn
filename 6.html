Wartortle

Water Type Pokemon Wartortle, the evolved form of Squirtle and the middle stage in its evolutionary line, is a Water-type Pokémon known for its sleek and formidable appearance. With its distinctive blue shell and tail adorned with a fluffy tuft, Wartortle exudes a sense of confidence and maturity that sets it apart from its pre-evolution.
