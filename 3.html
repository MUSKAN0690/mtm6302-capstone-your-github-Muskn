Charmender

Fire Type Pokemon Charmander is a Fire-type Pokémon known for its charismatic appearance and fiery personality. As the initial form of the Charmander evolutionary line, it holds a special place in the hearts of Pokémon trainers worldwide. Charmander resembles a small, bipedal reptilian creature with bright orange scales and a flame burning at the tip of its tail, a sign of its vitality and health.
